About Us

We are the platform That Believes in God

The Utulivu Space is a platform under the Love Generation Fellowship in Tanzania that connects, educates and motivates men and women on the love of God. Over the past 5 years, Utulivu Space has reached hundreds of people with the message of Utulivu (Peace), hope, and the love of God.

This has been achieved through various channels such as the digital space i.e. Podcasts, YouTube channel, zoom meetings, Instagram as well as physical meetings in the form of weekly fellowships, monthly services, and live events.

As such, Utulivu Space has attracted interest and frequent participation of people residing not only in Africa i.e. Kenya, Uganda and Nigeria but also globally including UK, US, and Australia. Through these activities, Utulivu Space has received ample testimonies on how their interaction with the platform healed and impacted their lives.
